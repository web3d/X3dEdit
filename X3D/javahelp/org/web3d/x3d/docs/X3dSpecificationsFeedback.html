Feedback: common principles

  • All specification comments go to X3D Working Group mailing list (linked above), where discussion is always welcome
  • All feedback mechanisms are listed at
  • Draft specifications and the bug tracker are accessible Web3D Consortium members only.

Common sources of feedback include

  • Open comments on publicly released specification documents
  • Member comments on working documents
  • Working group comments on preliminary draft documents
  • Interaction with formal partner organizations to achieve Web interoperability
